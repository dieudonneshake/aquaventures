About AquaVentures

Transforming lives through aquatic excellence

AquaVentures Academy is Rwanda's premier swimming education and pool management institution, dedicated to excellence in aquatic training and facility management.

Water Safety

Comprehensive programs to prevent drowning and promote water safety awareness nationwide.

Elite Training

Developing competitive swimmers for national and international competitions.

Sustainable Pools

Eco-friendly pool design and management solutions for Rwanda's growing hospitality sector.

Therapeutic Programs

Specialized aqua therapy for rehabilitation and special needs individuals.
